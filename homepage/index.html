Join A Metronome.

Almost every musician in the world right now is out of a job. Performing for crowds isn't safe and playing together online has always been a challenge because of latency.

Well, we can't overcome limitations of the speed of light so this is a work around to let musicians stay on beat while performing online.

JAM uses your device's internal clock to create a click track. Join a live stream and use the slider to correct for slight variations of each device.

So long as everyone knows the song structure, the audience will hear the band playing in unison.

The Steps:

Start an Instagram Live
Open live stream to sync the beat
Perform together for the interwebs!
