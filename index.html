About:


Almost every musician in the world right now is out of work. Performing for crowds isn't safe and playing together online has always been a challenge because of latency.

Well, we can't overcome limitations of the speed of light so this is a work around to let musicians stay on beat while performing online.

JAM uses your device's internal clock to create a click track. Use the slider to correct for slight variations of each device.

So long as everyone knows the song structure, the audience will hear the band playing in unison.
